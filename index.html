INTRODUCTION TO CAD/CAM AND CAE

With the advancement in technology, the automobile industry is striving for delivering products of increasing variety, user-friendliness, and higher quality in the context of increasing global competition. Industries cannot thrive in worldwide competition unless they introduce new products with better quality, at lower costs, and with shorter lead-times. With dramatic changes in computing power and the wider availability of software tools for design and production, engineers are now utilizing Computer-Aided Design (CAD) for Design, Computer-Aided Manufacturing (CAM) for Manufacturing, and Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE) for Analysis & Simulation systems to automate their design and production processes. These technologies are now employed daily for various engineering tasks with the objective to save time and increase production.

Our unique courses are specially tailored considering current industry demands and job requirements, which not only assist engineers in securing a job in the industry but also help them boost their design career by acquiring vast practical knowledge at the start of their careers.

Our training content is aligned with current industry practices, giving students an extra advantage during interviews as well as working independently on projects.
